# Netflix

> Streaming to 270M users — encoding, Open Connect CDN, chaos engineering

## What to Study

- Per-title encoding — using ML to compute optimal bitrate ladders per content type
- Adaptive Bitrate (ABR) streaming — DASH/HLS, buffer management, quality switching algorithms
- Open Connect CDN — Netflix's owned ISP-embedded appliances and how they reduce egress costs
- Microservices at scale — Zuul, Eureka, Hystrix and the patterns that came out of Netflix OSS
- Chaos Monkey origin story — how intentional failure injection became a reliability discipline

## Why This Matters for Platform Architecture

Netflix is the reference architecture for large-scale streaming and resilient microservices. Open Connect redefined how CDNs can be operated at ISP level, and the chaos engineering practice Netflix pioneered has become standard across the industry.
