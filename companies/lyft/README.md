# Lyft

> Ride sharing — compare architecture choices vs Uber

## What to Study

- Matching service design and how Lyft's approach differs from Uber's dispatch model
- ETA prediction pipeline and the data sources that feed it
- Pricing architecture and surge/Prime Time mechanisms
- Where Lyft's publicly documented architecture diverges from Uber's — use both eng blogs together

## Why This Matters for Platform Architecture

Lyft is most valuable studied alongside Uber — the two companies solved the same core problems with different architectural choices, making the comparison a study in how context and team history shape system design decisions.
