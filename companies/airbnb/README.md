# Airbnb

> Home marketplace — search, availability, pricing, trust

## What to Study

- Calendar and inventory management — availability queries under concurrent booking attempts
- Search relevance — combining geo, price, amenity filters with personalization ranking
- Dynamic pricing (Price Tips / Smart Pricing) — ML-driven suggestions to hosts
- Payment escrow model and disbursement timing
- Fraud detection and trust/identity verification pipeline

## Why This Matters for Platform Architecture

Airbnb's architecture demonstrates the complexity of a marketplace with long-lived inventory (calendar windows), trust requirements on both sides, and a pricing system that must balance host autonomy with demand signals — a pattern common to any two-sided marketplace with physical assets.
