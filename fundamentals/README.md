company study shallower.

## SRE Context

As a Sr Staff SRE, you have operational knowledge of many of these.
The goal here is to shift from *operational* understanding to *design* understanding.

For each concept, ask:
- How would I design a system that makes this property easy to achieve?
- What design choices make this property hard to achieve?
- What did I observe in production that was a consequence of a design choice here?
